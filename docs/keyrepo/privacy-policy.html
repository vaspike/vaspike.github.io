Privacy Policy

最后更新日期：2025年05月30日

Last Updated: May 30, 2025

KeyRepo致力于保护您的隐私。本政策概述了我们如何收集、使用和保护您的个人信息，以及您对这些信息的控制权。使用我们的应用即表示您同意本政策中描述的做法。

KeyRepo is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information, and your control over this information. By using our app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

收集的信息

Information Collected

KeyRepo设计为高度保护隐私的密码管理应用：

KeyRepo is designed as a highly privacy-protective password management app:

  • 本地存储的信息：您的所有密码和个人数据都使用高级加密存储在您的设备上，而不是我们的服务器上。
  • 不收集个人数据：我们不收集、存储或传输您的任何密码和信息到我们的服务器(当前MVP版本无需联网)。
  • Locally Stored Information: All your passwords and personal data are stored on your device using advanced encryption, not on our servers.
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your passwords and information to our servers (the current MVP version does not require internet connection).

数据使用

Data Usage

我们使用收集的有限信息：

We use the limited information collected:

  • 改进应用功能和用户体验
  • 诊断和修复技术问题
  • 了解用户如何与应用交互以优化界面
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues
  • To understand how users interact with the app to optimize the interface

数据安全

Data Security

保护您的数据安全是我们的首要任务。我们采取以下措施：

Protecting your data security is our top priority. We take the following measures:

  • 所有密码数据使用AES-256加密存储在您的设备上
  • 使用您的主密码作为加密密钥
  • 可选的生物识别认证(Face ID/Touch ID)
  • 自动锁定功能
  • 不将敏感数据传输到云端或我们的服务器
  • All password data is stored on your device using AES-256 encryption
  • Your master password is used as the encryption key
  • Optional biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID)
  • Automatic lock feature
  • No sensitive data is transmitted to the cloud or our servers

  • 当需要遵守法律、法院命令或法律程序时
  • 保护我们或他人的权利、财产或安全时
  • When required to comply with the law, court order, or legal process
  • When needed to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others
